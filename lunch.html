Garlic Herb Shrimp with Airfried Plantains

A delightful lunch that's both elegant and easy to prepare! Perfectly seasoned shrimp paired with crispy, golden airfried plantain slices, all tossed in aromatic herbs. This dish is packed with flavor and protein, making it an ideal midday meal that's both satisfying and nutritious. Ready in just 30 minutes, it's perfect for a quick lunch that doesn't compromise on taste!

Featured Recipe Details

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 medium plantains, sliced thin
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh herbs (thyme, cilantro, or parsley)
  • 3 tbsp corn oil
  • 1 lemon, for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400¬∞F (200¬∞C).
  2. Slice plantains thinly and spray lightly with oil. Season with salt.
  3. Air fry plantains for 12-15 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden and crispy.
  4. While plantains cook, season shrimp with minced garlic, fresh herbs, salt, and pepper.
  5. Heat corn oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Cook shrimp for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and cooked through.
  7. Serve shrimp alongside crispy plantains. Squeeze fresh lemon over everything.
