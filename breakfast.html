Perfect Breakfast Scramble

Start your day right with this delicious breakfast scramble! Fluffy scrambled eggs paired with warm tortillas and a fresh, colorful salsa. This protein-packed breakfast is quick to prepare, incredibly satisfying, and perfect for busy mornings when you need a nutritious meal that doesn't skimp on flavor.

Featured Recipe Details

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs, room temperature works best
  • 4-6 flour tortillas, warmed before serving
  • 2 medium fresh tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup white onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Make the salsa: In a small bowl, mix diced tomatoes, chopped onion, cilantro, and a pinch of salt. Set aside.
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl, add a splash of milk, salt, and pepper. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat with a little butter or oil.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture and let it sit for a few seconds.
  5. Gently scramble with a spatula, pushing eggs from the edges to the center.
  6. Cook until eggs are soft and fluffy, about 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat.
  7. Warm tortillas in a dry pan or microwave for 15-20 seconds.
  8. Serve scrambled eggs with warm tortillas and fresh salsa on the side.
